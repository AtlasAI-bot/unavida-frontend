Absorption, Distribution, Metabolism, Elimination (ADME)

Pharmacokinetics describes how the body handles a drug over time. In this section, students walk through each ADME phase and connect it to safe administration timing and monitoring responsibilities.

Section image placement (topic-relevant)
Quick Check #1
Which ADME stage is primarily responsible for biotransformation into metabolites?
✅ Metabolism (mostly hepatic enzyme activity).

What the Drug Does to the Body

Pharmacodynamics explains receptor interactions, dose-response behavior, and why the same medication can produce different outcomes across patients depending on context and physiology.

Quick Check #2
In one line: PK vs PD?
✅ PK = what body does to drug. PD = what drug does to body.
